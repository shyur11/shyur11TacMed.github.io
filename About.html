The primary focus of Our Ambulance Service Team is two-fold: The first is to reach people in an emergency situation as quickly as possible and administer life-saving first-aid on the scene. The second is to transport the sick or injured patient as quickly and safely as possible to the appropriate healthcare facility for further care within the golden hour.
